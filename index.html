Question 1 of 7

What does your ideal morning feel like?

Where are you? What do you see, smell, feel? How does your body feel when you wake up?

Question 2 of 7

What does your financial life look like?

Be specific. What is in your bank account? How does money flow to you? How does it feel to pay your bills?

Question 3 of 7

What does your work or purpose feel like?

What are you creating? How does your work flow? How does it feel to do what you do every day?

Question 4 of 7

How does your body feel in this life?

Your health, your energy, your beauty, how you carry yourself. What does it feel like to be in your body?

Question 5 of 7

What do your relationships feel like?

Your people. Your love. Your friendships. How does it feel to be surrounded by the right ones?

Question 6 of 7

What does your home and environment feel like?

Where do you live? What does your space feel like? What do you see when you look around?

Question 7 of 7
